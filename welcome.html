healthe Moms

We love and appreciate our Moms over at healthe and understand the crucial role they play in the overall well-being of Black families. We want to help, encourage, educate, and motivate you to be your healthiest self. Watch it become a lifestyle for the entire family!

About Us

Currently based out of San Francisco, healthe was built by Ebonne, a front end web developer and designer who desired to create a product to help Black families by teaching Moms about wellness. She advocates for inclusiveness, education, and empathy within the tech world.

Why healthe?

  • healthe was created with YOU in mind.
  • healthe helps you change your lifestyle from the comfort of your own home.
  • our healthe map helps you find local farmer's markets for cheap in-season produce.
  • healthe supports other Black wellness experts by linking you to external resources.
